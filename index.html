About ASHA Pura Studios

Pallavi, founder of Ashapura Studio, blends spirituality and contemporary art to create soulful, vibrant, and meaningful expressions.

Her studio offers paintings, murals, and décor that transform ordinary walls into timeless sanctuaries of peace and harmony, enriching interiors with elegance, creativity, soulful energy, and a lasting artistic impression that inspires mindfulness and emotional well-being. Each creation reflects Pallavi’s intuitive process, blending colors with consciousness. Her artworks not only decorate spaces but also elevate lifestyles, bringing balance, positivity, and a touch of timeless artistry.
